== Description ==
WooCommerce Quick View helps you set up a quick view pop-up and lets your consumers look at the image of your product faster and clearer without opening the product page. Also, you can customize the style of your site’s quick view option by going to the settings section.

Consumers can very easily navigate from one product to another. And one of the best parts of WooCommerce Quick View for WooCommerce is, you can also display the important details of your products like Price, Quantity, Customer Rating, Quality, Add to Cart button etc.


== Why Choose This Plugin? ==

Would you like to look at a small image of a product or a big and clear one? Of course, the big and clear one will be everyone’s preference!

But how often would you take the trouble to open a new browser page, wait till it’s loaded only for an image? Most people will not like to take the hazard to just look at an image and go for something easier.

That means most of the consumers might avoid your product only to avoid hazards. On the WooCommerce site, that’s a huge risk for any salesperson.

To forgo such a risk, We provide you with the best WooCommerce Quick View plugin you will find out there.

WooCommerce Quick View is a very lightweight and simple plugin but at the same time, it gives a professional-looking effect. Which makes your WooCommerce website attractive and helps your customer with a better and quick view of your products.
